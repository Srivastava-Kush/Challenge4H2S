[Gate Operations and Opening Times]
Stadium gates open 3 hours before kickoff. VIP and hospitality entrances open 4 hours before kickoff. Staff and volunteer entrances open 5 hours before kickoff. Gate MetLife is the primary public entrance. Gate Verizon serves east-side ticket holders. Gate Pepsi serves west-side ticket holders. Gate Bud Light serves north-side ticket holders and premium club members.

[Volunteer Roles]
Volunteers are stationed at all main corridors and gates. They wear bright turquoise vests with the FIFA 2026 Volunteer logo. Volunteers carry first-aid kits and transit schedules. They are trained to assist with crowd flow management, lost and found, and accessibility assistance. If you need help, approach any volunteer or ask the StadiumIQ assistant.

[Staff Escalation]
For issues requiring management attention, ask any steward to radio the Operations Centre. The Operations Centre is located on Level 2 near Section 208. Emergency escalations should be made to Gate MetLife Security Desk (extension 200) or Gate Verizon Security Desk (extension 201).

[Lost and Found]
The Lost and Found desk is located at Gate MetLife on the ground level. It operates during all event hours and for 2 hours after the final whistle. Items found inside the stadium should be handed to any steward or volunteer.

[Media and Press]
Press credentials must be arranged in advance through the official FIFA 2026 media portal. The press box is located on Level 3, accessible via the North Concourse media elevator. Unauthorized photography with professional equipment is prohibited.
