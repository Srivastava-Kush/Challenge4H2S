[Step-Free Access]
All major gates (Gate MetLife, Gate Verizon, Gate Pepsi, and Gate Bud Light) feature step-free, ramped entry points. The main corridors (South, East, West, North Concourse) are fully accessible to wheelchair users.

[Elevators and Lifts]
Lift 1 is located on the South Concourse near Section 140. It provides access between the ground level and the 100/200 level concourses. Only guests with accessibility wristbands, elderly guests, and staff are permitted to use Lift 1.

[Accessible Restrooms]
Restroom 1 is located on the South Concourse. It is fully ADA-compliant, featuring grab bars, emergency pull cords, and a lower washbasin. 
Companion seating and assistance dogs are welcome across all seating levels.
