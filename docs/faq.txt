[Stadium Information]
Lusail Iconic Stadium and MetLife Stadium are designed for FIFA World Cup 2026. Gates open 3 hours prior to kickoff. Re-entry is strictly prohibited. Smoking is banned inside the stadium bowl.

[Transport Options]
MetLife Stadium is served by NJ Transit rail service via the Meadowlands Station. Bus shuttle services run from Port Authority Bus Terminal. Main parking lots open 4 hours before kickoff. Metro services run until 2 hours after the final whistle.

[Food & Beverages]
Concessions are available at both 100-level and 200-level concourses. Halal, Kosher, Vegetarian, and Gluten-free options are available at Food Stand 1 on the South Concourse. Alcohol sales cut off at the 75th minute of the match.

[Volunteer Operations]
Volunteers are stationed at all main corridors and gates (Gate MetLife, Gate Verizon, Gate Pepsi, Gate Bud Light) to guide fans. Volunteers wear bright turquoise vests with the "Volunteer" logo. They carry first-aid kits and transit schedules.
