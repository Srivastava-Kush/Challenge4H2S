[Evacuation SOP]
In the event of an evacuation order, remain calm and follow volunteer instructions. Proceed to the nearest exit gate (Gate MetLife, Gate Verizon, Gate Pepsi, or Gate Bud Light). Do not use lifts or escalators. Wheelchair users should wait near designated refuge points for volunteer assistance.

[Medical Emergencies]
First Aid 1 is located on the West Concourse. Volunteers can assist in paging a paramedic. If you see a medical incident, report it to the nearest steward or submit an report through the app. Provide the section number or nearby column reference.

[Security Issues]
Report suspicious activity or unruly behavior immediately to security personnel. Text "FIFA HELP" along with your location and seat number to 88442 for assistance.
