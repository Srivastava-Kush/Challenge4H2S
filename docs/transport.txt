[Transport — NJ Transit Rail]
MetLife Stadium is directly served by NJ Transit via the Meadowlands Station on the Pascack Valley Line and Bergen County Line. Trains run from Penn Station New York and Hoboken Terminal. Travel time from Penn Station is approximately 45 minutes. Trains run every 30 minutes on match days. Last train departs approximately 90 minutes after the final whistle.

[Transport — Bus Services]
Express shuttle buses operate from the Port Authority Bus Terminal (42nd Street, Manhattan) on match days. Buses run every 15 minutes starting 3 hours before kickoff. Return buses run until 2 hours after the match ends. NJ Transit Bus Route 772 also operates from Secaucus Junction.

[Transport — Parking]
Parking Lots open 4 hours before kickoff. Pre-purchased parking passes are required and must be displayed on the dashboard. Tailgating is permitted in designated lots only. Accessible parking is available in Lot A (Blue Zone). Do not leave valuables in your vehicle.

[Transport — Ride-Share and Taxis]
Uber, Lyft, and taxi drop-off and pickup are at the designated rideshare staging area on the east side of the stadium (Gate Verizon side). Follow the green directional signs from Route 3. Estimated surge pricing is expected on match days; book in advance.

[Transport — Accessibility Transport]
Wheelchair accessible vehicles and ADA-compliant shuttle buses are available from the Meadowlands Station. Please contact the accessibility services desk at Gate MetLife on arrival to arrange personal assistance.
