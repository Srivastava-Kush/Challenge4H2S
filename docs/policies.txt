[Ticketing and Entry Policy]
All tickets must be presented digitally via the official FIFA 2026 app or printed in high quality. Ticket scanning occurs at all four main gates. Each ticket is linked to a specific gate and seat. Re-entry is strictly prohibited once you have exited the stadium. Lost or stolen tickets cannot be replaced on match day.

[Bag and Item Policy]
Bags larger than 12x6x12 inches are prohibited. Clear bags of the approved size are strongly recommended to expedite security screening. Prohibited items include: weapons, glass bottles, cans, selfie sticks longer than 12 inches, drones, laser pointers, and professional camera equipment with detachable lenses. Stadium security reserves the right to deny entry to any prohibited item.

[Alcohol Policy]
Alcoholic beverages are available for purchase at designated concessions on the 100 and 200 level concourses. All purchases require valid age verification (21+ in the US). Alcohol sales cease at the 75th minute of the match. Visibly intoxicated individuals will be denied service and may be removed from the venue.

[Prohibited Conduct]
Abusive, discriminatory, or threatening language is strictly prohibited. Entering the field of play will result in immediate ejection and possible arrest. Fans engaging in disruptive behavior will be removed from the venue. FIFA reserves the right to issue bans from future events.

[Medical and Special Needs]
Guests requiring medical assistance should notify the nearest steward or use the app to submit an incident report. Companion seating is available for guests requiring personal care assistants. Service animals are permitted in all areas of the stadium.

[Smoking Policy]
Smoking and vaping are strictly prohibited inside the stadium bowl, concourses, and all covered areas. Designated outdoor smoking areas are marked at Gate Pepsi and Gate Bud Light on the exterior plaza.
